Frequently asked

How do I get access after I join?
Checkout runs through LaunchPass. Once payment is processed, your Discord account is automatically linked to your AFL Premium membership and the private bet channels unlock in our server. You'll get a welcome message with onboarding instructions, and you can open a personal onboarding ticket so the team can confirm your unit sizing and bookmaker setup.
Do you guarantee profits?
No. There are no guarantees in sports betting. AFL Premium provides systems and strategies built around long-term edge, executed with discipline and proper bankroll management. Losing weekends happen and drawdowns are part of the process.
How much money do I need to start?
There's no fixed minimum. We recommend 1 unit equals roughly 1% of your bankroll. Our tracked bets use $50 units, so a $5,000 bankroll matches our tracked results 1:1. Smaller bankrolls scale down proportionally (e.g. $1 units on a $100 bankroll). If you're unsure, our onboarding team helps you size correctly.
Do I need accounts with specific bookmakers?
Ideally yes. Sportsbet, TAB, and Bet365 are the major Australian bookmakers we use. Having more bookies improves your ability to find the best odds and follow bets accurately. If you're missing an account or restricted, we'll help you organise alternatives during onboarding.
What has the performance been historically?
Five years of public tracking. Over 5,500 AFL bets logged. More than $42,000 in tracked profit at roughly a 19% ROI. Full historical and current performance is available on our public results dashboard.
Is this suitable for beginners?
Yes, provided you're willing to follow instructions and manage your bankroll properly. We recommend beginners start with smaller units and work closely with our team during onboarding. This isn't a get-rich-quick service. It's a long-term, disciplined system.
What happens during a losing streak?
Losing streaks are part of the process. Short-term variance is normal, even with strong edges. The system works because we don't change strategy after a bad round. We hold the line, track everything, and let the edge play out across the full season.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes, anytime. You can manage your subscription through your LaunchPass portal. Once cancelled, access remains active until the end of your current billing period. No lock-in.
What support is available?
Personal onboarding is included for every new member. Our team helps you set your unit sizing, organise your bookmaker accounts, and understand how to follow the package correctly from day one. Ongoing support is available through tickets and the active member community.
